Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Data We Collect

ClayBytes collects personal data that you voluntarily provide, such as your name, email address, and the content of your message.

In addition, because we use HubSpot CRM, certain technical information may be collected automatically through cookies or tracking scripts. This may include: IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to respond to inquiries, communicate about potential collaboration, and manage CRM records. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for advertising purposes.

3. Legal Basis

Data is processed under legitimate interest (GDPR Article 6(1)(f)): responding to inquiries, maintaining communication, and managing CRM functionality.

4. Data Retention

Emails and CRM records are retained only as long as necessary for communication, service delivery, or legal requirements. You may request deletion at any time.

5. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your data by contacting info@claybytes.nl. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Dutch Data Protection Authority (Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens).

6. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies and scripts provided by HubSpot. These are necessary for CRM functionality and analytics. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings.
